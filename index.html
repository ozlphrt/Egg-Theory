Simulation Rules
Egg Types & Strategies:
🟡 Yellow (Aggressive): Always attacks to steal energy. 🔵 Blue (Cooperative): Always cooperates and shares energy. 🔴 Red (Reciprocal): Mimics the opponent's previous choice. 🟢 Green (Random): Randomly decides whether to attack.
Game Theory Payoffs:
🤝 Soft & Soft (Coop): Both shapes gain +15 energy. 💥 Soft vs. Hard: Soft loses -15, Hard gains +15. 💢 Hard & Hard (Clash): Both shapes lose -10 energy.
How to Breed:
  • Shapes breed sexually when they collide on the board.
  • Both parents must have at least 70 energy.
  • If either parent has < 70 energy, they resolve a game theory payoff instead of breeding.
  • Shapes have a breeding cooldown of at least 5 encounters between successes.
  • Successful breeding costs 35 energy from both parents.
  • Total active shapes are capped at the grid cell limit (225).
How to Die:
  • Moving and existing consumes energy constantly.
  • If energy falls to 0, the shape dies.
  • A graveyard cross spawns and slowly decays over time.
